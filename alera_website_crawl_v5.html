About Alera

Alera Medtech: Innovation with Purpose.

Alera Medtech is committed to bringing real innovation to medical devices that clinicians and patients depend on every day, with a particular focus on neurology and vascular access.

The Challenge

In these high-stakes fields, many core devices used routinely (hypodermic needle electrodes, IONM electrodes, catheter securement devices, prefilled syringes, infection control caps) have changed very little over the past several decades. While other areas of patient care have made tremendous strides, these essential devices have not kept pace. The innovation has been incremental at best and stagnant at worst. We set out to change this.

How we follow our mission.

Focused Innovation

Target segments that serve as the foundation of neurological and vascular access but have seen limited meaningful progress.

Modern Design & Performance

Efficient Manufacturing

Scale manufacturing in ways that drive sustainable cost efficiencies.

Offer improved devices at affordable prices, making better quality accessible without any drop in standards.

What the evidence shows.

DPNCheck measures sural nerve conduction velocity and sensory nerve action potential amplitude at the point of care. These are widely accepted biomarkers of peripheral nerve function. The device's validity and reliability against conventional EMG-lab nerve conduction studies has been confirmed across multiple independent research groups, patient populations, and clinical settings.

ICC 0.97
